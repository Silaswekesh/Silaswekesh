About us

The plant was established in April 2022 with the objective of making and distributing concrete products, as well as other building materials nationally. The focus is on the supply of concrete products, sand, stones, timber coupled with offering transport services.

Vission

To be an excellent and competitive enterprise for the production of concrete products and distribution of building materials regionally and nationally, in Kenya.

Mission

To produce concrete products and distribute materials through provision of improved and quality products for buildings and associated structures by making competitive the construction industry in Kenya

Core values

Customer focus,Professionalisim,Intergrity
innovativeness,Partnership and Environmental consciosness

More About Sanowa Concrete Enterprise

The enterprise has invested in modern equipment including a modern Concrete Block making machine with accessories that guarantee full customer satisfaction. We also own a concrete mixer and transportation trucks for hire.


The enterprise high concern on ensuring health safety and environmental compliance. This is done through:

  • Ensuring environmental sustainability, health and safety of our employees, customers, suppliers and neighboring community
  • Compliance with applicable environment, Health and safety laws and regulations.
  • Continuous improvement of our HSE management systems, pollution prevention practices and safety programs.
